The [source](http://github.com/amrox/phillycocoa-site/tree/master) *and* [content](http://github.com/amrox/phillycocoa-site/tree/gh-pages) for [phillycocoa.org](http://phillycocoa.org) are now hosted on GitHub, using the [GitHub Pages](http://pages.github.com/) feature. Static HTML content is generated using [nanoc](http://nanoc.stoneship.org/) version 2.2.2, though we are considering migrating to [Jekyll](http://github.com/mojombo/jekyll). The site's design was intentionally [primitive](http://www.freecsstemplates.org/preview/primitive), but if you'd like to see a facelift, patches are welcome.
