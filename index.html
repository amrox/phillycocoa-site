CocoaHeads December 2010 Meeting - The Future of PhillyCocoa

December 7th, 2010.

Next CocoaHeads meeting will be Thursday, December 9th at Indy Hall.

This month we're going to continue the tradition of doing a year-end celebration and a look into the upcoming year. We'll leave room for General News and Show and Tell, but no (mini) talks.
