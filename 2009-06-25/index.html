CocoaHeads June 2009 Meeting Recap

June 25th, 2009.

Next CocoaHeads meeting is [Thursday, July 9th at Indy Hall Classic] (http://cocoaheads.org/us/PhiladelphiaPennsylvania/index.html). Please note that the date has changed from the previously announced date.

June 2009 Meeting Recap

  • We're talking about hosting a Philly satellite of iPhoneDevCamp 3, but we need help with planning and organization! If you want to help, please contact Andy Mroczkowski, or post on the mailing list.
  • Randy Zauhar demoed some cool stuff using OpenCL. If you want more information on his project, contact him.
  • Frank Szczerba (@fszczerba) posted the slides from his talk entitled, What’s New in iPhone OS 3.0, A Developer’s Perspective.

July 2009 Meeting Preview

  • Again, we'll be meeting on July 9th.
  • The developers from labs @ laan will talk about bringing TweetDeck to the small screen.
  • Other talks and presentations are welcome. Contact Andy Mroczkowski if you'd like to participate.
