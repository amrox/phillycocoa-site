CocoaHeads May 2009 Meeting Recap

May 19th, 2009.

There were some great presentations this month. If you didn't make it, you missed out!

WWDC09

A few items regarding WWDC:

The IndyHall Labs team is going to WWDC09!

Events and Parties SFMacIndie, Sunday June 7, 5-9pm iPhone Intelligence Party Monday June 8, 7-11pm * WebKit Party, Wednesday June 10 7-10pm

Watch the mailing list or follow us on twitter for further updates.

Show and Tell

First, Dave Martorana (@themartorana]) discussed how WebKit is used in Multiplex to rapidly create compelling interface elements.

Next, Mike Zornek (@zorn) showed his use of WebKit and the Raphaël JavaScript library for generating high-quality vector-based charts in his upcoming application, ProfitTrain.

Finally, Andy Mroczkowski (@amrox) demoed a technique that helps developers run a subset of unit tests in Xcode, as well as some testing basics.
