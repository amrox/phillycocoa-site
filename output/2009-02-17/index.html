CocoaHeads February 2009 Meeting Recap

February 17th, 2009.

Great turnout - thanks to everyone who participated!

Project Calculator Updates

By request, more comments were added, and variable names clarified in the existing Calculator source code. No functionality has changed. The source for the Calculator project can be found here.

Learning Cocoa

We are beginning to gather ideas about how to best serve the growing population of attendees who are new to Cocoa. A lightweight Cocoa “class” is being considered, but we need feedback on content, structure, and schedule from our target audience.

Please visit the PhillyCocoa Google group to contribute to the discussion.

Next Meeting - March 12th 2009

Our next meeting is March 12th, 7-9P and IndyHall. Far McKon is on schedule to discuss and demo some cool stuff using the Image Capture framework.
